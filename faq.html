Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our photo booth services

Why should I choose a photo booth?

Everyone is always looking for "something different" to have at their wedding reception or event. A photo booth does that by creating a buzz among the guests as they share laughs looking at each other's photo strips. Photo booths are great for everyone...young and old! They provide the opportunity for your guests to take interactive and candid photos that can be shared with everyone!

Does someone come to run the photo booth?

Absolutely! You will have at least one attendant to set up, run the booth, and answer questions throughout the event. They will also help guests with photos, scrapbooks, props, etc.

Are the photo strips printed on-site?

Yes, the photo strips are printed immediately. Be careful, there are some photo booth companies that do not print the photo strips on-site. Make sure you know what you are getting.

What kind of printer is used to print the photo strips?

The photo strips are printed immediately using a dye-sublimation (dye-sub) printer. Dye-sub printers have many advantages over ink jet printers. For instance, photos printed from a dye-sub printer will not smudge; therefore, no worries about getting ink on your formal attire! In addition, photos printed from a dye-sub printer will not fade over time like a photo printed with an ink-jet printer. The dye-sub printers produce photos that are comparable in quality to photos you would receive from a professional photo processing lab!

How many photos can we take during our rental period?

There is no limit to the number of photos you and your guests can take. With a simple push of a button, your guests will be able to choose between COLOR or BLACK AND WHITE PHOTOS!

How do I choose between a photographer and having a photo booth?

No need to choose! The services each provide are mutually exclusive. A photographer is primarily used for "formal" photos at a wedding ceremony/reception. For wedding receptions, most professional photographers are primarily there to capture photos at the ceremony and only a few at the reception. A photo booth is great for capturing photos throughout the reception. Memories and More Photo Booths will provide an interactive, informal, and fun photo experience! In addition, the photo strips will provide a memorable party favor for your guests.

Is there a mileage charge?

Typically not. There is no mileage charge for any event within 40 miles of Blaine, MN. This covers the Twin Cities Metro area.

Do our guests get to take any pictures home with them? And how long do they have to wait?

Yes, the photo strips are printed immediately on-site. Printouts take approximately 10 seconds!

Do you offer a Scrapbook Service?

Yes! We highly recommend a Scrapbook for weddings. Also great for birthday parties, anniversaries, or retirement parties. This is a great keepsake to have for years to come!!

Can I bring my own Scrapbook?

No problem. We will make sure people sign it and add pictures, whether it is your own or our scrapbook. If you choose to bring your own, make sure you have pens and adhesive if needed.

Do the guests have to pay to use the photo booth?

No. All the guests have to do is enter the photo booth and press a button. Their photos will be taken, and their photo strip will be printed immediately.

How many strips print out?

Two identical 2x6 photo strips are printed with each photo booth session. If more than 2 people are in the photo, we will print more so that each person gets one. There may be an extra fee for large school events due to every session having many people in each photo.

How big is the booth?

The Enclosed Photo Booth is approximately 8 feet long (front to back), 5 feet wide (side to side), and 7 feet tall.

How many people fit in the booth?

It depends on how cozy you want to be! The enclosed photo booth typically accommodates anywhere from 1 to 10 people. The Open Air Booth can fit quite a few, our record is 22!!

Is there a deposit required?

Yes. A deposit of $100 is required to reserve the photo booth.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as soon as you have your event date secured, especially for weddings and peak season events (May-September). Some dates book up to a year in advance, so it's best to secure your date early.

Can I customize the photo strip design?

Yes! Custom photo strip layouts are included in our Package #2 or can be added to Package #1 for $50. We can incorporate your names, event date, colors, and even logos or graphics to match your event theme.

What kind of space do you need for setup?

For the Open-Air Booth, we need approximately a 10'x10' space. For the Enclosed Booth, we need about 8'x5' plus additional space for guests to line up. We also require access to a standard electrical outlet within 25 feet of the setup location.
